Requirements

The following items will be required for the notarization process:

  1. Your NRIC (if Singaporean) and/or your Passport
  2. The original document(s) to be notarized
  3. If a notarized certified true copy is required, a photocopy of the original documents
    (together with the orignal)
  4. For notarization of company documents to be signed under the common seal or by its authorized attorney, the following additional documents:
    1. Board Resolution
    2. Company Constitution
    3. Power of Attorney
